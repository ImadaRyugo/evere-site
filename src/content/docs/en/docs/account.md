Evere works right away in guest mode without signing up, but registering an account protects your data and unlocks every feature. Here's how the two compare.

## Ways to start

When you open Evere, you can choose one of the following.

- **Continue with Apple**
- **Continue with Google**
- **Sign up with email** (tap the link in the confirmation email to activate)
- **Start as a guest** (no sign-up)

## What you can and can't do in guest mode

Even in guest mode, you can do the essentials: create projects, log expenses, see the settlement calculation, and use multiple currencies.

However, the following require an **account**.

- **Creating invite links** (to invite other app users)
- **Recording settlements** (marking things as settled)

:::caution
Guest-mode data is stored only on that device. **If you delete the app or switch devices, you'll lose access to it.** We recommend registering an account early to protect your data.
:::

## Switching from guest to a registered account

Even after you've started in guest mode, you can register an account anytime. **The data you created as a guest carries over when you register** — you won't lose anything.

Register from "Account" in the Settings tab.

## Benefits of registering

- **Your data syncs to the cloud** — access the same data after switching phones or from another device.
- **Share with someone far away** — even if your partner or friend is elsewhere, you can share the same project in real time.
- **Invites and settlement records become available** — the limits above are removed.

## Display name

The **display name** you set at the start is the name shown to your project members. Change it anytime from "Account" → "Profile" in the Settings tab.

## Managing your account and password

From "Account" in the Settings tab, you can:

- Change your display name
- (For email accounts) change your login password
- Check your plan, upgrade, and restore purchases
- Delete your account (all data is permanently deleted and cannot be undone)

Evere works with people who have the app and people who don't. There are two ways to add participants.

## Adding a participant by name

The simplest way is to **add a participant by name**. Enter a name when creating the project, or from "Manage participants" in project settings.

- You can add someone even if they don't have the app.
- Added participants can be selected when splitting an expense.
- Share the result with them via a [settlement report](/en/docs/settlement/#sharing-a-settlement-report).

Not everyone needs to install the app. Whether it's a couple or a friend who doesn't use the app, this approach gets the job done.

## Inviting members with an invite link

If the other person also uses Evere, you can invite them to the project with an **invite link**. Invited members share the same project in real time on their own device.

- Create an invite link from the project details and share it as a link or QR code.
- Invite links can be disabled, and may also expire.

:::note
Creating an invite link requires an account. It isn't available in guest mode. The person **joining** via the link also needs an account.
:::

## Which should I use?

- The other person doesn't use the app, or you want it quick → **add by name**
- The other person wants to log and check in real time too → **invite link**

You can also mix both within a single project.

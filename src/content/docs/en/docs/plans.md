## Free plan vs. Pro plan

Evere is free, and the essentials for splitting bills are all available. When you want to remove limits or add convenience, go Pro.

| Feature | Free | Pro |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Number of projects | Up to 3 | Unlimited |
| Receipt images | 5 per project | Unlimited |
| Multiple currencies & auto-conversion | ✓ | ✓ |
| Member invites & real-time sync | ✓ | ✓ |
| Manual exchange rate | — | ✓ |
| Budgets & threshold alerts | — | ✓ |
| Offline | — | ✓ |
| Ads | Shown | Hidden |

:::note
Check pricing and purchase plans (monthly, annual, lifetime) in the app. Pro may include a free trial.
:::

### Upgrading and restoring purchases

- Upgrade from "Account" or "Plan details" in the Settings tab.
- To carry a purchase over after switching phones, use "**Restore purchases**."
- Subscriptions are billed to your Apple ID, and can be managed or canceled anytime from your Apple subscription settings.

## Troubleshooting

### The settlement amounts don't match / someone is missing

An expense with an **unconfirmed exchange rate** is excluded from the settlement calculation. Check whether the settlement screen shows a warning, and confirm the rate on the affected expense ([Multiple currencies & exchange rates](/en/docs/currencies/#watch-out-for-unconfirmed-rates)).

### I can't add an expense

The project may be marked as **settled**. Unsettle it from the project list to add expenses again.

### I can't change the base currency

Once you log the first expense, the base currency can't be changed. To use a different base currency, create a new project ([Projects](/en/docs/projects/#currency-settings)).

### I can't invite or record settlements

These actions require an account. They aren't available in guest mode. Register an account from the Settings tab ([Account & data](/en/docs/account/)).

### I lost data / might lose it after switching phones

Guest-mode data is stored only on the device. Be sure to register an account and save your data to the cloud before switching phones.

### I want 4+ projects / to remove ads

These are solved by the Pro plan (see the comparison table above).

### Still stuck?

Reach out through the [contact form](/en/support/). You can also contact us from "Support" in the app's Settings tab.

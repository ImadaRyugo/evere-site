As you log expenses, the "Settlement" tab automatically shows "who pays whom, and how much." Leave the tedious split math to Evere.

## Reading the settlement screen

The Settlement tab shows the following.

- **Summary** — total number of expenses and total amount
- **Settlement status** — the amount and ratio of settled vs. unsettled
- **Payment comparison** — a chart comparing each participant's "amount paid" vs. "amount owed"
- **By currency / By category** — breakdowns by currency or category
- **Settlement** — the list of who should pay whom, and how much
- **Budget** — usage status, if a budget is set ([budgets are a Pro feature](/en/docs/features/#budgets-pro))

## Smart settlement vs. pairwise settlement

You can choose between two ways of displaying the settlement (switch it in settings).

- **Smart settlement** — aggregates debts and shows the arrangement that needs the **fewest number of transfers**. Fewer back-and-forths to finish settling.
- **Pairwise settlement** — shows who owes whom, per pair, as-is.

## Marking things as settled

Once the money has actually changed hands, tap the item on the settlement screen, confirm, and mark it **settled**.

- Settled amounts are excluded from further calculations.
- The states are "settled," "partially settled," and "unsettled."
- Made a mistake? **Unsettle** to revert.

You can manage settlement at three levels of granularity.

- **Per expense** — mark an individual expense as settled
- **Per settlement flow** — mark settled by the "who pays whom" line on the settlement screen
- **The whole project** — mark it all settled at once (see [Projects](/en/docs/projects/#marking-a-project-as-settled))

:::note
Recording settlements (marking things as settled) requires an account. It isn't available in guest mode. See [Account & data](/en/docs/account/).
:::

## Sharing a settlement report

Choosing "Share settlement report" on the settlement screen shares the result as formatted text. Paste it into a group chat and even people without the app can see the result.

When sharing, you can choose:

- **What to share** — result only, or with the expense history
- Whether to include participant info and receipt images
- Whether to add a converted amount in a sub-currency
